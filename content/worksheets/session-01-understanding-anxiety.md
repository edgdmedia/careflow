# Session 1: Understanding Anxiety

## What is Anxiety?
Anxiety is your brain's alarm system — it's designed to keep you safe. But sometimes the alarm goes off when there's no real danger.

## Your Anxiety Profile
- Where in your body do you feel anxiety?
- What situations trigger it?
- What thoughts come up?

## The Anxiety Cycle
Trigger → Thought → Physical Sensation → Behavior → Short-term Relief → More Anxiety

## Practice: 5-4-3-2-1 Grounding
- 5 things you can see
- 4 things you can touch
- 3 things you can hear
- 2 things you can smell
- 1 thing you can taste

## This Week's Challenge
Notice your anxiety without judging it. Just say: "There's anxiety. That's okay."
