# Session 2: Naming Your Anxiety Triggers

## What Are Your Triggers?
Triggers are situations, people, places, or thoughts that activate your anxiety.

## Trigger Journal

## Common Trigger Categories
- Social situations
- Work/performance pressure
- Health concerns
- Uncertainty
- Conflict

## Practice: Trigger Mapping
Draw a simple map: Write "Me" in the center. Around it, write your triggers. Connect them to how you respond.

## This Week's Challenge
Identify one trigger each day. Just name it — don't try to fix it.
