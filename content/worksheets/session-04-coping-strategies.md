# Session 4: Building Coping Strategies

## Your Coping Toolkit
Anxiety often feels less overwhelming when you have a small set of strategies you can
reach for quickly. The goal is not to make anxiety disappear instantly, but to respond
to it with more steadiness and choice.

## Notice What Helps
- What has helped you calm your body before?
- What helps you feel grounded when your mind is racing?
- What makes things worse?

## Coping Strategy Categories

### Body-Based
- Slow breathing
- Stretching
- Walking
- Drinking water
- Progressive muscle relaxation

### Mind-Based
- Naming the thought
- Challenging the thought
- Reminding yourself: "This feeling will pass"
- Focusing on what is true right now

### Environment-Based
- Stepping outside
- Reducing noise
- Calling a safe person
- Moving to a calmer space

## Practice: Build Your Personal List
Write 3 strategies that help your body, 3 that help your mind, and 2 that help you feel safer in your environment.
