# Session 3: Challenging Anxious Thoughts

## Thought Distortions
- **Catastrophizing** — Assuming the worst will happen
- **Mind Reading** — Assuming you know what others think
- **All-or-Nothing** — Seeing things in black and white
- **Fortune Telling** — Predicting negative outcomes

## The CBT Triangle
Thought → Feeling → Behavior

## Practice: Thought Record
1. What happened?
2. What thought went through your mind?
3. What distortion is this?
4. What's a more balanced thought?

## Reframing Exercise
Anxious Thought: "Everyone will think I'm stupid."
Balanced Thought: "I'm here to learn, just like everyone else."

## This Week's Challenge
Catch one distorted thought each day and write a balanced alternative.
